Meet Our Team

Ruth Ramabu

Biokineticist

Ruth Ramabu completed her Diploma and B-tech degree at Tswhane University of technology, she then furthered her studies and completed a health science honours degree in Biokineticis at WITS. She is registered with the Health professional council (HPCSA) and Biokineticist Assocoation of South Africa (BASA). Ruth has a long history of sport and conditioning working for TUT High Perfromance Basketball and Netball, as well as working for the Midrand Back and Neck Centre and Physio and Wellness centre. She is also affiliated with non profit organisation Olieven Development Centre (ODA) as a health specialist. ... Ruth is a hard working empathetic individual who believes that exercise is medicine and is passionate about educating everyone about the benefits of exercise and conditioning especially in development and elderly population and people with chronic and special population. She was inspired to become a biokineticist due to her love for sport and exercise as a child and unfortunately needing rehab herself due to injury. Her experience with her own injuries and still having to go for treatment and living a healthy lifestyle to maintain her fitness helps her tackle her job with compassion and understanding of pain management. More pain, no gain. Less pain, more gain. Re educating ourselves about progress loading, leading to long term health and wellness -Ruth Ramabu
