Bio-kinetics = Life through Movement

Ever heard of a biokineticist?

A biokineticist is a healthcare professional who specializes in exercise therapy and rehabilitation. They assess, treat, and prevent a variety of physical injuries and conditions through exercise and movement. Biokineticists work with patients to improve their physical well-being, rehabilitate injuries, manage chronic diseases, and enhance overall health and fitness.

What we do

Biokineticists use exercise as a primary tool to help patients improve their strength, flexibility, endurance, and overall functional capacity. They design individualized exercise programs tailored to each patient's needs, taking into account their medical history, current fitness level, and specific goals. These programs often include a combination of cardiovascular, strength, flexibility, and balance exercises.
